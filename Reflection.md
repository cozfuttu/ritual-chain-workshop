# Reflection: Random Selection in Bounties

Random selection removes human bias and voting collusion from winner selection. It's ideal for raffles, lotteries, or any bounty where quality is subjective but fairness is paramount.

**Public vs Hidden:** Submissions stay hidden until reveal; randomness generation is transparent and verifiable on-chain.

**AI vs Human:** This variant removes AI and voting – winners are selected by randomness. This is the most decentralized but assumes all submissions are equally valid.

**Trade-off:** Random selection is fair but doesn't reward quality. Best for situations where participation itself is the reward.
